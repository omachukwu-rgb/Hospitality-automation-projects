# Hotel Review Management Automation

## Overview
An automation system that helps hotels manage and respond to guest reviews efficiently.

## Problem
Hotels often struggle to respond to reviews consistently due to limited staff time.

## Solution
This workflow captures reviews and assists with generating timely responses.

## Tools
- n8n
- Airtable
- OpenAI

## Benefits
- Faster review responses
- Better guest engagement
- Reduced manual workload
